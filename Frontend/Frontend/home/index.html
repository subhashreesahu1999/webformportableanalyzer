We are Our Story

WHO ARE WE ?

Active since 2000, Saloni was started by founders who really care about personal and institutional reputation in furniture sector.

Saloni has been conducting careful research and development for all 19 years it has been in the business since its establishment in order to present durable, quality, functional and innovative furniture to its customers and all consumers.

We are one of the biggest modern couch manufacturers in Turkey.

Saloni manufactures and sells armchairs, couches, corner seating groups, bedroom suites, dining room sets, wing-back chairs, wall units, coffee tables, and accessories in furniture sector. Saloni keeps creating the latest trends and providing you with the highest quality products with great passion with its trained and informed staff. The number of our stores is constantly increasing since we started business. Saloni keeps growing with dynamic vendor structure, trained manufacturing and management staff, and post-sale staff who have adopted high satisfaction principles.
